The Mad Scientist

In the rift of battle, where champions clash,
Amidst the fray, there stands a man of gas,
A mad chemist, with laughter on his lips,
Singed, the one who leaves a trail of dips.

He brews his potions, concocts his schemes,
And with a cackle, unleashes his dreams,
A poison cloud, that spreads and creeps,
His enemies caught, as they stumble and weep.

With his flask, he lobs his deadly brew,
And the poison hits, burning through and through,
His laughter echoes, as he runs away,
Leaving behind, a trail of decay.

But Singed is not one to shy away,
He charges in, with his fling and sway,
He's a master of chaos, a wild card,
And when he's on the field, expect the bizarre.

His mind may be twisted, his motives unclear,
But one thing's for sure, he's a force to fear,
Singed, the chemist, the madman of the rift,
A champion who's impossible to predict.

Can you escape The Mad Scientist?
